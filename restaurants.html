Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB):

The CPCB, a statutory organization, works under the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change. They undertake various projects to control and prevent water pollution. While they don't directly accept donations, supporting associated NGOs or contributing to government environmental funds can help their efforts.b. State Pollution Control Boards (SPCBs): SPCBs work at the state level to prevent and control water pollution. Donations to associated NGOs or contributing to state-level environmental funds can support their efforts.
